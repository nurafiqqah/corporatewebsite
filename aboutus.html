The establishment of this campus started from the idea of the Minister of Higher Education, Dato 'Seri Vice Chancellor of UiTM and also the Rector of the campus to increase the number of students.

The establishment of UiTM Kota Campus was announced by the Minister of Higher Education, YB Dato' Mustapa Mohamad on 18 October 2006 and the first session his studies began in July 2007.

The establishment of the Kota Campus takes into account several criteria among which is the location is in the urban area of Kota Bharu and must be strategic for the purpose of image enhancement.

Following the announcement, a City Campus Establishment Committee chaired by the Campus Director was formed. This committee is composed of heads of divisions and units at UiTM Kelantan. Wisma KUB which has 4 floors with a total floor area of 24,522.75 square feet has been selected as the temporary campus of UiTM Kota Campus or its official name Universiti Teknologi MARA Kelantan, Kota Bharu Campus.

In 2008, the Executive Committee Meeting, Universiti Teknologi MARA Malaysia subsequently approved the lease of 2 3-storey building blocks with a total floor area of 19,200 square feet which was operational in July 2009.
